About Me

Hey there! Welcome to my site. My name is Adam Brunn and I am a self-taught software developer of 8 years. My first significant project was when I built Mod-Tools for Xbox 360 gamesaves. I am currently in a web development boot camp, hosted by the University of Texas, where I plan to hone my developer skills to transition into a full time web development career.

If there is one thing you should know about me, it's that I am a problem solving junkie. My passions outside of software development include philosophy, gaming and becoming the best me. I am a cronic internet hipster, being an early supporter of many now-viral internet services. These services include Spotify (2012), Discord (Alpha 2015), Twitch.tv (2012), and games like League of Legends (Pre-Season 2010) and Minecraft (Alpha 1.1 2010).
